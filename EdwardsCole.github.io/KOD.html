KOD bio

KOD is J Cole's fifth studio album.The KOD acronym could be interpreted three different ways- Kids On Drugs, King OverDosed or Kill Our Demons. The album highlights drug use, both literally and figuratively. These drugs include not only cocaine and alcohol, but also love and money. He also continued his focus on black America from previous albums.
