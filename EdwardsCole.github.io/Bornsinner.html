Born Sinner

Born sinner debuted in 2013, it allowed for the commonly underrated artist to show his process as an artist and his struggle with meeting the expectations of his timeless role model and fellow artist NAS. Additonally, he used this album as a way to speak about how he persevered through his personal obstacles. The mood of the album transitions from dark and to hopeful which truly shows that even those that are held to a high standard, experience downfalls that they need to pick themselves up from.

Extra info

J Cole became much more involved in the music industry in 2019. He finished his KOD tour and jumped right back into making music, including his own and features.
